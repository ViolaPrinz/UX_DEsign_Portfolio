InkTank

A app for tattoo entusiasths and tattoo artsits

Overview

Ink Tank is a app for tattoo enthusiasts and tattoo artists. The mission from Ink Tank is that users can find what they are looking for in the tattoo world, for example a specific tattoo design, which they got already in their mind or a certain specialized artist. The next tattoo studio close by or the next tattoo convention is easy to find with InkTank. Furthermore InkTank is for adventurous people who love travelling and like to get a tattoo on their travel or as an tattoo artist to do tattoos while travelling.

Discovery and Research

Competitive Analyis

To learn to know the market I conducted a Competitive Analysis. Like this I could find out what users expect and what could eb missing in the current products

User persona

After I conducted interviews, with people who got a relation to tattoos I knew more about the potential users and about the tattoo world. To make the collected data more visible and clear, I created User Personas.

❮ ❯

User Journeys

With the insights I had gained from the User Personas, I created User Journeys to show off their goals and the way, they likely would take to accomplich their goals.

Information Architecture

Sitemap

The next step, was to build the information architecture of the app. This I started to accomplish with a card sorting session. I get great insights about what subjects users think are matching together, so that I could confidentally build a Sitemap.
